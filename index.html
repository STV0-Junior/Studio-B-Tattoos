About Our Studio

Excellence in Tattooing Since 2015

Studio B Tattoos was founded with the mission to create high-quality body art in a professional and safe environment.

Our artists are highly skilled and specialise in various styles, from traditional to realism, ensuring each client receives personalised and unique work.

We prioritise hygiene, safety, and customer satisfaction in all aspects of our work. Our studio is state-licensed and follows strict sterilization protocols.

Visit us to experience the perfect blend of artistic talent and professional service.
